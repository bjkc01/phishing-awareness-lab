# Security Awareness Strategy

A strong security awareness initiative complements technical controls by addressing the human element.

## Strategic goals

- increase employee recognition of phishing patterns
- reduce risky behavior through training
- improve reporting rates for suspicious emails
- establish repeat offender remediation

## Program pillars

1. **Baseline assessment**
   - simulate phishing campaigns
   - capture click and submission behavior
2. **Targeted training**
   - assign follow-up training based on observed risk
   - provide general awareness for low-risk users
3. **Reporting reinforcement**
   - encourage use of phishing report channels
   - track who reports suspicious messages
4. **Continuous measurement**
   - calculate campaign metrics over time
   - adjust training cadence based on results

## Key outcomes

- lower phishing susceptibility
- higher reporting engagement
- faster risk remediation
- deeper security culture awareness

## Ethical considerations

This project only uses fictional data and simulated scenarios. It is designed to train defensively and avoid real-world harm.
