# Targeted Remediation Training

This training module is intended for employees who triggered higher-risk behaviors in the phishing simulation.

## Learning objectives

- identify real-world phishing indicators
- understand consequences of credential submission
- practice safe reporting and verification
- improve resilience against social engineering

## Targeted training plan

- review sample phishing emails in `templates/`
- complete scenario-based exercises
- participate in simulated response walkthroughs
- receive follow-up coaching from the security awareness team

## Follow-up actions

- assign mandatory refresher training within 7 days
- schedule one-on-one awareness review if repeat behavior is observed
- track improvement in future campaign results
