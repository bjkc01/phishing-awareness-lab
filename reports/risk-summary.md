# Risk Summary

## Campaign risk profile

The simulated phishing campaign identifies the following risk profile for the fictional organization:

- **High-risk behaviors**: credential submission and repeated campaign interaction.
- **Moderate-risk behaviors**: link clicks without reporting.
- **Low-risk behaviors**: users who reported suspicious items or did not interact.

## Organizational impact

Phishing susceptibility increases the probability of account compromise and unauthorized access even when technical controls are in place. Employee behavior is a critical defensive control.

## Risk reduction focus

- improve phishing awareness training completion rates
- reinforce the use of reporting channels
- prioritize targeted remediation for repeat offenders
- align future campaigns with MITRE ATT&CK T1566 to improve threat modeling
